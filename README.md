 PROPOSAL 1: FOR FEED THE FUTURE (NPO) WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT

Prepared by: Shaun Ramoshaba
Technology stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
 
1.	Organization overview
Name: FEED THE FUTURE (NPO)
FEED THE FUTURE is a registered Non-Profit Organization (NPO) based in Johannesburg, South Africa. Established in 2015, the organization provides daily meals to primary school learners in underserved rural areas. Currently they rely on a Facebook page, which limits their credibility for securing Corporate Social Investment funding and transparent reports to local and international donors. This project aims to bridge this digital gap 

2.	Website goals and objectives 
The primary goal is to establish a professional digital face for the NPO.
Specific objectives include:
•	Establish credibility: Create a 6 page professional website for official NPO operations
•	Volunteer recruitment: automate volunteer registration to increase the database by 30%
•	Donor transparency: Use an “impact gallery” to showcase community success stories

3.	Current website analysis
Currently, the organization has no official website. Analysis reveals:
•	Fragmented information: Details are scattered across social media posts
•	No central repository: No official platform for NPO registration certificates or tax-deductible receipts
•	Low global visibility: The lack of an SEO-optimised site prevents global funding opportunities

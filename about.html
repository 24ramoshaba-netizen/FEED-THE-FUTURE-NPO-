About FEED THE FUTURE

Empowering the next generation through nutrition and care.

Our History

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Johannesburg, FEED THE FUTUREstarted as a small Community soup kitchen. Our founder noticed that many primary school learners were attending classes on empty stomachs, directly affecting their ability to concentrate and succeed in school.

What began with 20 meals a day has grown into a structured NPO that supports over 15 schools across the Gauteng province.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A South Africa where no child is deprived of an education due to hunger.

Our Mission

  • To provide daily, balanced meals to learners in underserved areas.
  • To educate communities on sustainable food gardening.
  • To partner with local farmers to source fresh, organic produce.
